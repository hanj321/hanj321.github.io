## Texas Tech

### IE 5304: Biomechanics & Work Physiology  
**Role:** Instructor of Record  
**Term:** Spring 2026  
**Format:** Hybrid (In-person & Online)  
**Enrollment:** 11 students  

Graduate course introducing biomechanical principles and physiological responses relevant to human work. Topics include muscle function, anthropometry, posture, joint moments, lifting, pushing and pulling, fatigue, and physical work capacity, with applications to ergonomic assessment and design.

## Virginia Tech

### ISE 4624: Work Physiology  
**Role:** Instructor of Record  
**Term:** Fall 2023  
**Format:** In-person  
**Enrollment:** 36 students  

Undergraduate course in work physiology covering physiological responses to physical work, fatigue, energy expenditure, and applications to ergonomics and human performance.

### ISE 3614: Human Factors Engineering and Ergonomics  
**Role:** Instructor of Record  
**Term:** Summer 2023  
**Format:** Online  
**Enrollment:** 7 students  

Undergraduate course introducing fundamental principles of human factors engineering and ergonomics. Topics include human capabilities and limitations, anthropometry, physical and cognitive ergonomics, workplace design, and evaluation of human–technology systems.
